About Us

Roshan Holdings Ltd is a dynamic and customer-focused company providing a comprehensive range of automotive and retail services. The company operates a modern tyre centre, offering a variety of high-quality tyres, wheel alignment, and balancing services to ensure vehicle safety and performance. Complementing this is a fully stocked convenience store, catering to everyday shopping needs with groceries, snacks, household essentials, and more.

Roshan Holdings Ltd also features a wines and spirits shop, supplying a diverse selection of premium alcoholic beverages for different tastes and occasions. Additionally, the company runs a state-of-the-art petrol station, offering high-quality fuel, lubricants, and other automotive fluids to keep vehicles running smoothly. To enhance customer convenience, the petrol station is equipped with a service bay, providing professional vehicle maintenance, minor repairs, and servicing.

With a strong emphasis on quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, Roshan Holdings Ltd continues to be a trusted name in the automotive and retail sectors, ensuring that both motorists and shoppers receive exceptional service under one roof.
